Places to Stay in Town

The Yearly Family Festival

The annual Family Festival is one of the largest local events of the year and pulls people in from every corner of the county. With a funfair, live music on the town platform, daily events and non-stop family fun, theres no place to be than ballinamore during this festive week.

CowBoys and Heroes

Cowboys and Heroes, Ireland's No.1 Country Music and Americana Festival, with a full line up of the best Irish Country Music Acts alongside an American Car Show, Western Horse Shows, Country Karaoke, Line Dancing and Jiving classes and competitions,Country Star Singing Competition Finals, five bars, onsite Saloon Bar and Restaurant, Food and Market Stalls and lots more. This is undoubtedly the largest event in the year for the humble town, attracting people for all counties with is huge venue and not-worthy performnces. The event is located just outside the town and and one wouldnt be wrong to think theyve gotten lost in a bustling city while this festival is on.
